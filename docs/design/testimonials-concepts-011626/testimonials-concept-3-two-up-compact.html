CONCEPT 3: TWO-UP COMPACT GRID

Layout: 2-column grid on desktop, stacked on mobile

Design decisions:

• Compact cards with small SVG quote icons

• Text truncation (4 lines) keeps cards uniform height

• Location displayed under name (adds local credibility)

• Border separator between quote and author

• Maintains brand colors: sage, terracotta, cream

Tradeoff: Less room for long testimonials, but balanced density

Best for: 4 testimonials with local geographic appeal
