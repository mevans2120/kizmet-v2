Contemporary Wellness: Fraunces + Plus Jakarta Sans

Fraunces is a modern variable serif with "wonky" optical sizing that adds personality while staying refined. Plus Jakarta Sans is geometric and confident with excellent legibility. This pairing feels fresh and contemporary while maintaining warmth and approachability for a wellness brand.
