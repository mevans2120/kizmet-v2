Design Rationale

  • Exact logo match: Font sizes (24px body, 30px initial), weights (500/400), letter-spacing (-0.01em), and overlap (-16px) match the website navigation exactly.
  • Left-aligned layout: Both sides now use left alignment, creating a cleaner, more modern reading flow that matches how people naturally scan information.
  • Gradient accent bars: Top bar (front) and left bar (back) use a sage-to-terracotta gradient, incorporating all brand colors in a subtle, cohesive way.
  • Subtle botanical accent: The olive branch motif suggests natural healing and growth without overwhelming the design.
  • Gradient divider: The line separating contact info from address uses the brand gradient at low opacity.
  • Cream background: Warmer than white, feels more inviting and less clinical. Appropriate for a healing practice.
