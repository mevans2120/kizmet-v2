Design Rationale

  • Stacked logo treatment: The "Kizmet / Massage" wordmark with oversized K and M matches the website. On sage background, it uses cream/white tones; on the back, it uses the standard charcoal/sage colors.
  • Vertical orientation: Stands out in a stack of horizontal cards. Creates a more modern, distinctive impression that suggests confidence and individuality.
  • Bold color blocking: Large sage area dominates the front, making the brand color unmistakable. Creates strong visual memory - "the green card."
  • Split layout: The cream section anchors Destiny's name separately from the brand, giving her personal presence while maintaining brand hierarchy.
  • Terracotta accent line: Small but impactful detail that adds warmth and draws the eye to the name. Prevents the design from feeling too cold/corporate.
  • Corner triangle on back: Subtle callback to the sage field on front, maintaining visual continuity without overwhelming the contact info.
  • Ample line spacing: Contact items have breathing room, making them easy to read and reference quickly.
