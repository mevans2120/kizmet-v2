Design Rationale

  • Stacked logo treatment: The "Kizmet / Massage" wordmark with oversized K and M matches the website exactly. On the back's cream strip, it's rotated vertically for a distinctive touch while maintaining brand consistency.
  • "Third Generation Healer" badge: Immediately communicates the unique story and heritage. This differentiator is the first thing people see, setting Destiny apart from other massage therapists.
  • Gradient organic border: Terracotta-to-sage transition represents the passing of healing knowledge through generations. Feels handmade and intentional, not corporate.
  • Paper texture overlay: Subtle noise pattern adds tactile quality, suggesting the card should feel special in your hands. Consider printing on textured stock.
  • Concentric circles motif: Symbolizes healing energy radiating outward, or the layers of holistic wellness. Understated watermark that adds depth without distraction.
  • Dark sage back: Inverts the color scheme, creating visual interest between front and back. The cream strip with vertical logo is a memorable design choice.
  • Warm cream (not pure white): The slightly warmer tone (#f5efe6) feels more organic and aged, like well-loved paper. Supports the heritage narrative.
  • Terracotta icons on dark background: The warm accent color ensures icons are visible against sage while maintaining the earthy palette.
