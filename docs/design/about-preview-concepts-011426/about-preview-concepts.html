About Preview Component

Three concepts for a homepage component that teases Destiny's story and encourages users to visit the About page

Concept 01

Quote-Led Editorial

Leads with a compelling pull quote that hooks visitors emotionally. Minimal layout puts focus entirely on Destiny's voice. The quote creates intrigue and invites deeper exploration.

Meet Your Therapist

"

In my family, healing was never something you learned from a textbook. It was passed down through touch, through presence, through care.

— Destiny, Third-Generation Healer

Read My Story
Design Rationale
Quote as Hook The quote is the most emotionally resonant content from the About page. Leading with it creates immediate intrigue about Destiny's unique background.
Minimal Layout No image or extra elements competing for attention. The typography does all the work, creating an editorial, magazine-like feel.
Soft CTA "Read My Story" is inviting rather than demanding. The outline button style keeps focus on the quote while still being clickable.
Concept 02

Split Layout with Image

Classic two-column layout with image on left, content on right. More informational approach that previews credentials alongside the story teaser. Good for users who want facts before clicking through.

Design Rationale
Visual Balance The image creates visual interest and puts a face to the name before users even click. The 50/50 split feels balanced and professional.
Credentials Preview Showing credentials as pills gives users quick validation. "Third-Generation Healer" is unique and creates curiosity.
Story Teaser The intro paragraph is the actual first paragraph from the About page, creating continuity when users click through.
Concept 03

Generational Timeline

Visualizes the "third-generation healer" story as a timeline. Shows the lineage from grandmother to mother to Destiny, making the heritage tangible and creating a unique differentiator from other massage therapists.

Unique Differentiator No other massage therapist in the area likely has this story. The timeline makes it the centerpiece of the brand identity.
Summary

Comparison

Concept 1: Quote-Led

Best for: Emotional connection. Lets Destiny's voice do the work. Minimal and elegant. Good if you want an editorial, magazine-like feel.

Concept 2: Split Layout

Best for: Balanced information. Shows face + story + credentials. More traditional layout that users expect. Good for building trust quickly.

Concept 3: Timeline

Best for: Unique differentiation. Makes the heritage story visual and memorable. Most distinctive approach. Good for standing out from competitors.
