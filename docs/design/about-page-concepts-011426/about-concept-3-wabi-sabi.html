I am Destiny

A massage therapist who believes that the body carries wisdom. My work is to create space for that wisdom to speak, and to guide the release of what no longer serves.

01

The Path Here

I came to massage therapy through my own experience of pain. A climbing accident left me searching for relief that traditional medicine couldn't provide.

What I found instead was something deeper: a practice that connects body to mind, practitioner to client, suffering to ease. The transformation was so complete that I knew I had to share it.

Ten years later, I've worked with hundreds of bodies. Each one has taught me something. I continue to learn, to study, to refine my understanding of what healing truly means.

My Approach

Three principles guide every session. They are simple, but their practice is a lifetime's work.

01

Presence

Before technique, before knowledge, there is presence. I bring my full attention to each session, each moment, each response your body offers.

02

Listening

Your body speaks in tension patterns, in breath, in subtle movements. My hands have learned to listen. Together, we discover what you need.

03

Space

Healing requires space: physical space for tissues to release, mental space to process, emotional space to let go. I hold that space for you.
