Meet Your Therapist

Where Healing Meets Intention

I'm Destiny, and I've spent the last decade learning to listen to what bodies need. Every session at Kizmet is a collaboration between your goals and my expertise.

My Story

  • Licensed Massage Therapist, WA State
  • Pacific NW School of Massage Graduate
  • Advanced Deep Tissue Certification
  • Prenatal Massage Specialist
  • 10+ Years Clinical Experience

The body never lies. It holds our joy, our stress, our trauma, and our triumphs in its tissues. Learning to read those stories has been my life's work.

My journey began unexpectedly, following a rock climbing accident that left me with chronic back pain. Traditional medicine offered limited relief, but massage therapy opened a door I didn't know existed. That transformation was so profound that I knew I had to share it with others.

After graduating from the Pacific Northwest School of Massage, I apprenticed with practitioners across multiple modalities. Each teacher added new tools to my practice: the precision of deep tissue work, the gentleness of Swedish techniques, the intuition of craniosacral therapy.

Today, I draw from all of these influences to create sessions that are uniquely tailored to each person who walks through my door. No two bodies are alike, and no two sessions should be either.

My Approach

Four principles guide every session at Kizmet

01

Deep Listening

Every session begins with conversation. Understanding your goals, your history, and your current state allows me to tailor each treatment to exactly what you need today.

02

Intuitive Touch

Beyond technique, I've developed an intuitive sense for what each body needs. Sometimes that means deep pressure; sometimes it means gentle holds. I let your tissues guide me.

03

Whole Person Care

I treat the whole person, not just isolated symptoms. Tension in your shoulders might stem from stress at work, poor sleep, or an old injury. Understanding the full picture leads to lasting relief.

04

Empowered Healing

My goal is to help you understand your own body better. I share stretches, self-massage techniques, and lifestyle insights so you can continue your healing journey between sessions.
