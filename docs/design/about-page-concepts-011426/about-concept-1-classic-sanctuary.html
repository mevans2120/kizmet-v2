My Journey

  • Licensed Massage Therapist
  • 10+ Years Experience
  • Deep Tissue Certified
  • Swedish Massage Specialist
  • Prenatal Massage Certified

My path to massage therapy began after experiencing firsthand the transformative power of healing touch during my own recovery from a sports injury. That experience ignited a passion that has guided my career ever since.

I trained at the Pacific Northwest School of Massage, where I developed a deep understanding of anatomy, physiology, and the art of therapeutic touch. Since then, I've continued to expand my skills through advanced certifications and workshops.

Every client who walks through my door brings a unique story. Some seek relief from chronic pain, others need recovery support after an injury, and many simply crave a moment of peace in their busy lives. I consider it a privilege to be part of each person's wellness journey.

My Approach

Every session is tailored to your unique needs, drawing from multiple modalities to create your ideal treatment.

Listen First

Before any session, we'll discuss your goals, concerns, and any areas that need special attention. Your feedback guides every aspect of our work together.

Holistic View

I treat the whole person, not just symptoms. Understanding how stress, posture, and lifestyle affect your body helps me address root causes, not just surface tension.

Lasting Results

My goal is to provide relief that extends beyond our session. I'll share stretches, self-care techniques, and lifestyle suggestions to support your ongoing wellness.
